Data in Use
Access and Approvals
Restrict access to a 'need to know' basis. 僅限需知道的人員存取
Information Owner or approved designee must be an additional approver for access to information systems containing data. (e.g. internal human capital, accounting) 此類資訊需經資訊所有人或其指定人員核准後使得存取
Information Owner or approved designee must establish access rights / distribution requirements for data. (e.g. share drives, collaboration sites, direct data access) 資訊所有人或其指定人員需建立存取權限清單
Information Owner or approved designee must review access rights to data periodically or when classification levels change. 資訊所有人或其指定人員需於資料分類規則變更時審核存取權限，亦需定期檢視此清單
Information Owner or approved designee must approve removal of data from Firm premises. 須由資訊所有人或其指定人員核准後才能移除資料
Information Owner or approved designee must approve sharing of data internally. 須由資訊所有人或其指定人員核准後才能對內公布資訊
Information Owner or approved designee must approve sharing of data externally. 須由資訊所有人或其指定人員核准後才能對外公布資訊
Non-disclosure or confidentiality agreement must be in place with individuals or third-parties prior to sharing data. 必須與廠商或個人簽署機密資訊等協議後才能共享資訊
Background checks, where legally permissible and training for PwC employees and staff must be completed prior to granting access to data. 必須經過背景調查且經過訓練的PwC員工才能取得權限
Marking / Labelling
Removable storage media must include a label on the exterior of thecontainer.

Label must not be easily removable and include at a minimum: • Asset number as assigned by IT, details for return using a general support phone number and must not indicate the PwC company or brand on the label (e.g. NOT include PwC name, address, individual contact information) to prevent from highlighting a sensitive asset.		 資料若儲存於行動儲存裝置須於外觀標示標簽。

標簽須至少符合以下要件：由GTS賦予其資產編號並標示電話號碼，不得包含任何PwC資訊、(如:PwC、資誠、公司地址、任何個人聯絡資訊 )。
All electronic data (e.g. PwC reports, spreadsheets, PowerPoint and PDF documents) must be labelled in the metadata per classification level and include distribution limitations in the footer, where applicable. (e.g. Internal Use Only, Confidential Document) 所有電子文件(PwC reports、Excel、 PPT、PDF…etc.)須於頁腳或任何地方標示其文件等級（如內部使用，機密文件）
Clearly mark classification on physical medium. 實體文件需清楚標示文件等級
Photocopying
Permission from the Information Owner should be obtained prior to making any copies or taking any photos (e.g., using phone, camera, or other device) of data.

Highly confidential data to be distributed should have a unique identifier for each recipient the data is shared with.		 在製作任何副本或攝影照片（例如使用手機，相機或其他設備）前取得所有人許可

需發佈之高度機密需備賦予獨立的識別碼
Copies of the data follow the same storage and handling requirements as the originals. 資料副本亦需遵循正本所有規定
Use of collaboration tools to share data must be controlled to include only authorised participants. (e.g. recommend using controls like webex passcodes/passwords) 使用協作平台分享資訊需運用方法設定其存取權限(如：密碼)
Printing, Scanning or Faxing
Data must be printed, scanned or faxed from and to individuals using a secured ID or within a secured area. 列印或傳真或掃描，需有身分識別機制或在安全的區域執行
Receipt confirmation of faxed or remotely scanned or remotely printed data sent externally to an individual must be acknowledged. 列印或傳真或掃描文件至外部人員後須作確認
Printers, scanners, and fax machines must not retain the data upon completion of job. 傳真、列印、掃描設備，在完成工作後不得保留文件
Voice
Be aware of surroundings when reading, discussing, or otherwise communicating data in public places (e.g., airplanes, restaurants, elevators, cell phone conversation, hangouts, etc.). 討論、閱讀資訊時需隨時注意四周環境，避免資訊被旁人竊聽
Refrain from leaving messages

containing data on answering machines or voicemail systems.		 禁止留言相關資訊於任何Voicemail
語音留言不得保留超過7天
Conference call discussions around data must include only authorized participants (e.g. recommend using controls like web ex passcodes/passwords) 控管參與電話會議之人員(建議使用WebEx或是使用密碼)
Foreign Travel
Refer to Member Firm local travel policies where applicable. 參考各Member Firm Local Policy

Data in Motion
E-mail Exchange
E-mail attachments must be encrypted using approved methods (e.g., WinZip with 256-bit, PGP, or minimum 128-bit encryption, etc.).

Highly confidential data must not be included in the body of an email message to prevent mistaken distribution.		 Email附件需加密

高機密資料不得直接放置於Email信件內文
Passwords for encrypted files should be provided through a separate channel other than email (e.g. phone, fax, SMS text message) and follow PwC minimum password complexity requirements defined in the Global ISP. 加密資料的密碼不得使用同樣管道傳送(如信件壓縮檔案的密碼，可透過電話，簡訊傳送，不得使用Email)
E-mail should only be sent to named individuals and not to group E-mail distribution lists or addresses. Email收信人欄位只能是單一個人，不能為群組
Transport (General)
Data sent outside of the PwC network should be transferred using a secure file exchange service or process (e.g. PKI enabled website, client hosted secure FTP, MFT- 2Go) 需使用安全的檔案傳輸方式將資訊傳送至外部(如： PKI enabled website, client hosted secure FTP, MFT- 2Go)
Must only take hard copy documents from office if transferred directly between office/home/client. (not via pub, bar, gym, restaurant, etc.) 如自辦公室往返家中或客戶所在地，請僅攜帶文件之副本
Pickup, receipt, transfer, and delivery of information media must be restricted to authorised personnel or approved courier. Automated tracking and accountability controls should be used. 資料須經由已授權人員取得、接收、傳送、轉移。資料（包裹）需有自動追蹤機制。
Must obtain client approval to transport hard copy or electronic copy data from client site. 經手轉移任何客戶資料(軟硬體資料)都須經過客戶核准
Delivery of hard copies be sealed in a package with a return address provided and sent only to designated authorized individuals who confirm receipt of delivery. (e.g., via phone call, individual mail, signature, etc.). 實體資料須封存於含有Return地址的包裹，接收時須由指定授權人員接收，並需要有確認的動作。
Refer to local member firm secure workspace and acceptable use policies for additional data handling requirements. 參考各Member Firm Local Policy

Data Storage / at Rest
Storage on Information Systems (File shares, databases, etc.)
Data must only be stored in approved information systems and locations (e.g., SharePoint sites, LN databases, share drive folder, etc.). 資訊需存至核准的系統內(如SharePoint sites, LN databases, share drive folder, etc)
Only authorized individuals may have access to data in information systems, related backups and access be reviewed periodically as appropriate. 只有經過授權的人能存取資料，且需定期查核備份的存取及狀況
Storage on Removable Media (USBs, CDs, Backup Tapes, etc.)
Only approved removable media devices can be used to store data and data must be protected with an approved encryption method. (e.g. PwC encrypted USB drives (memory sticks) or WinZip encrypted files for CDs and DVDs.)

*Additional client approval and restrictions may apply.		 只能透過認可的裝置存取資料，且需有加密保護(如PwC加密過的USB，or CD內的winzip)

*如有客戶要求也須符合客戶之存取條件
Password protected device, file or media must be used. 儲存裝置需有密碼保護
Must not use portable media as permanent storage as should be stored on system of records. Delete contents of memory sticks before and after use. Copy to PwC network any files created on smartphones or tablets. 不得使用可移動裝置作為永久存取之存放位置。使用後須清除內容。
Storage on Personal or Mobile Devices (Smart Phones, Tablets, Laptops, etc.)
Device must be protected with password or PIN with forced timeout set to lock and secure device when not in use. 行動裝置密碼需有嘗試次數上限，超過將會鎖定或是清除。

Unattended devices must be powered down or in hibernate mode when outside the office or end of use. 當下無人使用的設備於所外或不使用時需關閉或休眠
Must use PwC IT approved systems and equipment 須使用PwC IT認可的系統及裝置
Retention, Disposal, and Destruction of Stored Data
Unless subject to legal hold, electronic information required as a record (to evidence work undertaken on engagements or otherwise to support the operations of the firm) should be transferred to and stored on the appropriate system of record and deleted at the end of its specified retention period. 除法律因素，電子資訊需轉移至並存儲在適當的系統中，並於指定的時間後刪除。
Unless subject to legal hold, duplicate or superseded versions of records and electronic information that is not a record should be deleted at the end of the engagement or, at a maximum, two years after its last modification. 除法律因素，電子資訊之附本需於專案完成後，或最後修改日兩年後刪除。
